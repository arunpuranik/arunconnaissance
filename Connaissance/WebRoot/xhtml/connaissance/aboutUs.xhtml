Connaissance Group

Connaissance means acquaintances and we believe acquaintance is not just knowing someone but knowing enough for sustainable happiness.

With our genre of entrepreneurship, we feel creating happiness is creating true value. Those who would disagree say, it’s Shareholder Value. Easy as it might seem, this art is the most difficult science. We therefore say that while we innovate to create the best products and services, we are actually in the business of manufacturing ‘Happiness’.

This is what we attempt to do with our every single decision here and that’s why we say ‘Where Employees enjoy. Customers Rejoice’. Manufacturing happiness through our business activities therefore would remain the central objective of our organisation. We wish that our philosophy carries unique space and is completely removed from the chores of the existing organisational practices.

The physical asset of Connaissance may just be over 200 Cr. but the business value of our ideas is immeasurable. Our only aim is to spread happiness, through the collective strength of our intellect, vision, strategies, ideas, hardwork, efficient execution, practices and processes. Connaissance Group has interests in three core sectors of Realty, Education and IT.

Our areas of business

1. Realty - Connaissance Realty is a major player in the realty sector in Karnataka engaged in landscape development, constructions, and other real estate activities of buying, selling, leasing and consulting for mega projects.

2. Education - Connaissance Education is engaged in education in K-12 category at the moment with its school, Shri Ram Vidyalayawhich is one of the reputed schools in Karnataka. We run the school under charity. Very Soon we will be coming with a research wing too, for developing the teaching pedagogy with IT integrations, which will be called as SRE labs.

3. IT – We believe that IT is an art. This art form requires a deep sense and science of imagination.

• Training - We are an authorised partner for SAP Global Training & Certification program. Presently we have our SAP education centre in Bangalore and will be coming up in Hyderabad & Chennai in next 3 Months

• Resourcing

• Consulting & Support - In SAP Consulting we have multiple projects of customers like Kurlon, Unichem, etc.

• Product & Platform development

• Applications around content

 

Lakshmana Nagaraja Setty

An entrepreneur, who believes entrepreneurship, is not just about creating wealth but about creating well being and therefore he does not just want to measure the success of his enterprise by the growth of business but also by the growth in well being of employees and customers. This also lies at the core philosophy of his enterprise Connaissance.

His is a typical rags to riches story of a successful entrepreneur who takes the plunge into entrepreneurship just with the dream and meagre resources and lives through the journey of trials and tribulations just with the intensity of vision as the sole asset to create a successful and sustainable business platform that shapes and structures the enterprise and the lives of people within.

He left a prolific career in IT and returned from US to make his beginning as an entrepreneur from a very small office carved from the space below the staircase from where his construction venture started in Jayanagar, Bangalore. Today the business and the real estate assets are in crores through multiple projects that are underway but the roots still remain as a reminder of that humble beginning.

The initial years of his career after Engg. where he was a rank holder was into manufacturing of machine tools where he handled multiple challenges in production, business and business growth and grew very fast in hierarchy to Head International business and become its CEO and his experience became the pedestal for envisioning the complexities of creating and establishing an enterprise which he did through his venture Connaissance Asset Shoppe (I) Pvt. Ltd.

Today the Realty venture spans many projects in land development in Mysore and Bangalore and construction venture in Devanahalli, near Bangalore International Airport. The vision is to transform the realty space by introducing state of art technology for a zero defect in quality of construction and expand to various part of the country as a trusted name in realty business. As housing is becoming an important mission of the government to provide homes to all, his vision is to align to this mission and look for opportunities that helps exponentially grow the realty business.

Being an engineer and technology as a space close to his heart, he started his IT venture by becoming an authorised partner for SAP Education in 2014. SAP is the de-facto technology standard of the world today and the most sustainable with immense possibilities of growth in future. The company aims to become the largest partner for SAP Education in the country and grow into areas of SAP consulting in future to touch atleast a 100 Cr. in revenue in the next three years and a business of atleast 500 Cr. as a whole with construction and other ventures.
