Training Formats

There are three methods of training in SAP.

ILT (Instructor Led Training Program) +

This format of training will happen with Instructor in the classroom in which the training is conducted. So if the classroom happens in Connaissance, the trainer will be in the classroom in Connaissance. There are some modules for which Instructor Led Training can be conducted in Connaissance, like ABAP, CO, SRM, CRM, PS, PM, QM and many other modules.

VLC (Virtual Live Classroom) +

In this format of training Instructor conducts the training from one central place for a particular module for the students across various partner location. Right now there are five modules for which only VLC mode of training is available namely, FI, SD, MM, HR and PP. The trainer normally operates centrally from SAP office and all the partner locations conducting these modules are connected to the trainer. There are more than one trainer incase the number of participants are more across locations. The candidates for the training can chat, raise hands, talk to the trainer over phone if they have doubts or wish to clarity anything. It will have live video link shortly.

Even though in VLC mode, trainers may not be provided directly by partners as per SAP policy but to make the experience better and unique and to show how much value we associate to customer satisfaction, we are seriously considering giving an additional trainer support based on the economics of the program. At Connaissance w`e consider every proposal that can significantly increase value to customers.

OLT (Online Training Program) +

In this format of training program, there is no instructor and the learning happens through an online mode through a Learning Management System which is an application in which all the topics are arranged in a form of lesson plan. On clicking a lesson plan, the ppts open up with the voice over each ppt to make the participant understand the topic clearly. Incase of any doubts, there is a doubt pad to register the doubt which is then handled by a Subject Matter expert who explains by email, call or remotely accessing the computer of the participant and explaining the doubt. The OLT training can happen on anyday including Sunday and holidays except National Holidays, between 7 am in the morning to 9 pm in the night.

This mode of training is likely to get phased out soon.

Modules and Methods of Training

1. Learning Hub Model – It is a cloud based learning solution in which more than 145+ courses are available. It is a guided learning program and does not require any instructor but can be provided by the SAP Education partner. An internet access is required to access the learning hub. A student gets a user Id and password and can learn the module with simulations of all the configurations and the transaction screens. This is the latest offering from SAP and the most popular as it gives anytime, anywhere, any device access. Global certification from SAP Germany can be written after completing the module.

2. Site License Model – This is a dongle based learning solution from SAP and contains only 5 modules, FI, MM, SD, HR and ABAP. In this model internet access is not required. The dongle is attached to the computer/laptop and the student accesses the content of the training. Once completed the student can write the Global Certification program.
